Our Services

Whatever your Building Maintenance needs are, we have the skills, equipment and professionalism to get the job done hassle free at a competitive price.

Window Cleaning

Campark Difficult Access Services specialise in rope access techniques (abseiling window cleaning) enabling our window cleaners to clean windows that would normally be extremely difficult to access. With no height restrictions, our teams of abseiling window cleaners are the perfect solution to cleaning buildings of extreme size and height.

Pressure Clean

We are experts in pressure cleaning and facade cleaning and can take care of all your needs. We are highly experienced pressure cleaners providing a superior clean at a very competitive rate. We have set ourselves exacting standards and all work is done carefully and thoroughly. All projects are overseen and inspected post clean.
